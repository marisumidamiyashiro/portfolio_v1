Process & Insight

Step-by-Step

We begin by listening to each podcast, taking notes, brainstorming ideas and sketching our designs out. In this stage we do not have to worry about how we'll be tying each book together, but rather to make sure our designs give the consumers an idea of what the story may be about.

After narrowing down our sketches to one per book cover, we begin to experiment with different mediums for one design to see what would work beest. It's important to keep your options open and play around. After finalizing your design concept with a chosen medium we begin working on building up our last two books.

This part may be the most challenging. You want each design to stand on its own yet appear to be part of a set. This can be done by way of color, type, medium chosen, or overall layout.
